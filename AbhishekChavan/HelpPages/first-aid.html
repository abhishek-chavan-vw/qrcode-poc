First Aid Tutorials

How to Treat a Cut

Step 1: Wash the wound with clean water.

Step 2: Apply antiseptic solution.

Step 3: Cover it with a clean bandage.

How to Perform CPR

Step 1: Check if the person is responsive.

Step 2: Begin chest compressions (30 times).

Step 3: Give 2 rescue breaths, repeat until help arrives.
